On your taskbar, select the Nvidia control panel

Click on "Adjust desktop color settings" link on the left panel

In the main panel:
Under "1. Select the display you would like to change."
Select the iPad screen (probably called Apple Color LCD)

Under "2. Choose how color is set."
Click on "Use NVIDIA settings"
Color channel -> All channels
Brightnes -> 50%
Contrast  -> 50%
Gamma     -> 2.4

Under "3. Apply the following enhancements:"
Digital vibrance -> 50%
Hue              -> 0

Click on Apply (bottom right of the window)
Close the Nvidia control panel.
